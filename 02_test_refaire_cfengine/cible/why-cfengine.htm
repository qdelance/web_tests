Why CFEngine

The best Configuration Management Solution if you need:

✔  The Fastest and most Scalable Solution
✔  To know the Actual State of your Systems
✔  The Solution with the Best Security Track-record
✔  Full Flexibility and Granular Control
✔  A Mature and Enterprise ready Solution

Download the whitepaper

  

✔ The Fastest and most Scalable Solution

CFEngine Agents execute within 1-2 seconds. Within 5 minutes you can roll-out a global change regardless of size. The responsiveness is uniquely fast. The largest deployment of CFEngine runs on more than 100,000 servers.


✔ Know the Actual State of your Systems

With CFEngine you will get information about the Actual state on your system, and not only the intended state. CFEngine Agents probe the system every 2.5 minutes by default, and frequent report collection ensures that you at
all times will be able to see what the current state of all your machines, services and settings are.


✔ Best Security Track-record

CFEngine has very few third party dependencies. Since its inception back in 1993 less than a handful of security vulnerabilities have been published. That's almost 20 years of excellent track-record, and a big reason why CFEngine is so popular among industries like finance, government, oil and gas.


✔ Full Flexibility and Granular Control

CFEngine gives you more granular control than any other Configuration management Solution in the industry. Our System Engineers have  full control to automate their systems at whatever level they want. CFEngine Agents operates at file-level, and conduct changes in the actual files as opposed to doing snapshot comparisons and whole file-replacements. Thanks to abstractions (called Bundles), it is easy to elevate policies to a high-level language.


✔ A Mature and Enterprise ready Solution

CFEngine has been running in large-scale production environments for more than 15 years. If you want a thoroughly tested solution that is built on technology agnostic concepts to ensure your configuration solution will continue to work tomorrow, CFEngine is a safe bet.
